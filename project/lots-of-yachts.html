Lots of Yachts, Lots of Spots

Brief: Lots of Yachts, Lots of Spots organizes and hosts events of all kinds aboard beautiful yachts in West Palm Beach. I was approached by the CEO who needed a new website and online presence to compete in the difficult South Florida market.

Execution: I quickly put together a new website using the Wix platform, focusing on capturing beautiful images of all of the boats available for use. I also assisted in setting up social media accounts and integrating them to the site, as well as Google Adwords review.

Tools: Wix, Adobe Photoshop, Google Adwords
