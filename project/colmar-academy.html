Colmar Academy

Brief: Looking to improve my skills, I enrolled in the Codecademy introductory course, Build Websites from Scratch, a nine-week intensive course aimed to introduce the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. As part of my final project, I coded a design mockup for a fictional secondary school website, Colmar Academy.

Execution: This was a wonderful opportunity for my to showcase my new coding skills, as I had to build the website from scratch. I enjoyed the challenge of it, and particularly enjoyed the design process of choosing colors, fonts, and transition effects.

Tools: HTML, CSS, GitHub
