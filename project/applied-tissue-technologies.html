UI/UX Design Projects

Applied Tissue Technologies

Brief: I was asked to design a website mockup for Applied Tissue Technologies, a medical device company that offers a number of products, their flagship product, a micrograft device for treating skin wounds.

Execution: Examining their current site, I put together a design that would streamline the user interaction. Part of the issue is that the company owns two websites with very similar colors and logos which link back and forth to each other. This makes it difficult for the user to find the information they need. My design approach focused on clarifying all the available information.

Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
