The Paley Foundation

Brief: The Paley Foundation is a 501c charity that provides assistance to families and children undergoing limb reconstructive surgery, as well as provide programs, publications, and public outreach events to spread awareness and knowledge of innovative orthopedic procedures. The foundation supports medical mission trips to disadvantaged countries, medical conferences, and educational initiatives.

Execution: I work with the Paley Foundation to manage their social media channels, and assist with website design and content writing as needed. Moving forward the foundation plans to expand its reach and impact, and this will include increased social media engagement, and new website and branding.

Tools: HTML, CSS, Kindful, Buffer, Adobe Photoshop
